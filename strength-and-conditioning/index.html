Strength and Conditioning


Monsters of the Midway

Following fall cuts, Chicago weather begins turns sour and inhospitable to outdoor ultimate. The A-team trims practice and separates into small "pods" to begin a powerlifting regimen catered to the field athlete. We pride ourselves in our adherence to the most basic tenet of modern athletic training: lift heavy, lift often.

Fitness pods continue in the weightroom until mid-winter, at which point they refocus on short sprints, cutting, light plyos, and conditioning for the upcoming series. By the time sectionals rolls around, our players are big, fast highlight factories.

Interested in our lifting regimen? Take a look.

Pod Leaders

The A-Team leadership selects the best ambassadors of both of pumping iron and of throwing plastic to lead pods. In each of our 5-6 pods we pair a lifting leader and a throwing leader who complement one another's repertoire. A throwing session follows each lift in which members of the team hone their disc skills at the direction of their pod throwing leader.
