Tryouts


A-team

The A-team is the highest level of ultimate at UChicago, representing the University in the USA Ultimate DI College Division, and as such represents a serious commitment.

After cuts, A-teamers have 2-3 days of practice and 2-3 days of fitness and throwing "pod" per week until the season ends, as well as 3 pre-series tournaments and the college series. Usually the A-team fields a roster of between 23 and 26.

B-team

The B-team, though a rising program in the developmental division, does not carry the same rigor in performance and commitment that the A-team does. Attendance to practice, tournaments, and pod is not required for B-team membership, and anybody interested in men's Ultimate at UChicago may participate in B-team practice. The B-team roster varies between 10 and 30 bodies throughout the year.

Tryout Process

Tryouts occur in the first half of fall quarter and consist of 2 practices per week and 2 tryout tournaments. We do not expect attendance at all events, though if you wish to make the A-team, ensure that we see you play enough to make an informed decision on your tryout.

We offer late tryouts to select individuals demonstrating the clear ability or potential to be a contributing A-team member and who missed fall tryouts. If you are interested and believe you are qualified, please contact the captains at chicagoultimate@gmail.com and explain why you belong on the A-team.
